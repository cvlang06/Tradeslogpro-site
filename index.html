Management

Your tools go on the truck every morning and come back every night — or they're supposed to. TradesLog Pro's Van Load-Out system makes sure nothing gets left on site and every job gets the right gear.

  • 🗂 Create job-type profiles — Interior Paint Day loads differently than Exterior
  • ☀️ AM check: confirm tools loaded, log paint colours and quantities per site
  • 📍 Assign specific tools to individual job sites in a session
  • ✅ Return check: sign everything back in at end of day
  • 🔁 Smart Recall remembers your last load-out and suggests it next time

Built for the real
working day

⏱

Smart Time Entry

Log start, end, breaks, job, task and phase — with AI Task Learning that remembers your descriptions and auto-suggests them. Specialty OT toggle bypasses the weekly threshold on the fly. Per-shift rate overrides, Sunday premium, repeat shifts, and site photo attachments.

⚡

OT Engine

Automatically splits your hours into Regular and Overtime across a Monday–Sunday week, respecting the 40h threshold. No manual math — ever.

📋

Logbook & Job Summary

Every shift searchable and filterable by period, job, or keyword. Swipe between periods. Group by job to see total hours and pay per site. Export CSV or PDF anytime.

🧾

Professional PDF Invoices

Generates a full invoice with your business info, client details, work summary table, reg/OT labour breakdown, billable expenses, and GST — sent by email or saved to cloud.

📸

Receipt OCR Scanning

Powered by Google ML Kit — photograph a receipt and the app reads the amount and suggests the category automatically. One-tap Quick Scan from anywhere. No manual entry, no lost paper.

🏦

Business Tax Vault

A completely separate ledger that cleanly splits tax-deductible overhead from job-linked costs. Total deductions front and centre. Export a PDF Tax Summary to hand straight to your accountant.

📊

Earnings Stats & Projections

Year-to-date P&L, period profit and loss, job leaderboard, daily earnings trend chart, and projected year-end based on your current pace. 5-year growth history.

👥

Client Manager

Store client company, contact, email and address. Invoice PDFs pull client info automatically — no retyping the same details every time.
